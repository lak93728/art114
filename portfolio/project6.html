Level Up with HACC

Client: Harrisburg Area Community College (HACC)

Description: A lightning‑bolt logo created for HACC's Level Up program, symbolizing starting strong and finishing stronger. The design extends into a t‑shirt featuring a sky of bolts leading to graduation imagery. A promotional poster completes the set with a proud student looking up toward her success.
